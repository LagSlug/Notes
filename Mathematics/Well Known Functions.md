
### Absolute Value

<u>Properties</u>
- non-decreasing
- non-negative

<u>Formula</u>
$$
\begin{aligned}
	f(x) = &\|x\| \\ 
	&\|x\| =
	\begin{cases}
	x,  & \text{if $x \geq 0$} \\
	-x, & \text{if $x < 0$}
	\end{cases}

\end{aligned}

$$
